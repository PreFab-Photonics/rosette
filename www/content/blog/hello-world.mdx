This is the Rosette blog. We'll use this space to share what we're building, why we're building it, and what's coming next.

Rosette is still early. There's a lot of ground to cover and a lot of rough edges to smooth out. A blog felt like the right way to think out loud as we go.

## What to expect

We plan to write about:

- **Releases**: what changed and why it matters
- **Design decisions**: the tradeoffs behind the tools we ship
- **Guides**: practical walkthroughs for common workflows

We'll keep posts short and concrete. No filler.

If you want to follow along, the best way right now is to watch the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/prefab-photonics/rosette). More to come.
